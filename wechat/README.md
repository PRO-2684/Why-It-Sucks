# Why WeChat Sucks

<details><summary>Voice call is grouped under video call.</summary>

![call](./1-call.png)

</details>

<details><summary>You need invitation to join groups larger than 200 members.</summary>

![invite-to-join](./2-invite-to-join.jpg)

</details>

<details><summary>There's no cloud chat history.</summary>

![no-cloud-history](./3-no-cloud-history.jpg)

You'll have to upload your copy of chat history to share with others. Maybe there IS cloud history, but it's not available to users.

</details>

<details><summary>Stupid "group note" feature.</summary>

![group-note](./4-group-note.png)

This is used to count participants in a group. The idea is that members can send a message indicating that they'll participate, and the tool'll gather a list of participants. But it clutters the chat history a lot and notifies everyone just like any other message.

</details>
